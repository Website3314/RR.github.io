Why I prefer Notepad over Word

Many people see me typing down notes on note pad and they're always like : Being hippy are we? Are you weird? Why do you use notepad? You got something against microsoft?
Firstly, I love Microsoft, I own a Microsoft Surface Pro. To be honest there was an issue with the Word software; whenever I opened it, it would get stuck and go 'Windows has detected a problem... troubleshoot?' Troubleshoot is useless and I couldn't be bothered to repair it so I just used notepad instead.

Another reason is, notepad opens faster than word. Normally while waiting for Word to open I could procrastinate a bit or listen to music which I didnt really have a problem with but sometimes while working I get these sudden ideas that I just have to write down somewhere so I'd open notepad and note it down since it opened much faster.

I got used to writing things on notepad, so I used it instead of word (unless I need to make a pdf of something.)

Notepad is also really simple. You open it, you type. Word is kinda intimidating with its pages and word counter and that scary assistant that pops up every time you open it. (What's its name, pin?) I heard that pin was fired from the newer versions of word, but still. Word makes me feel too responsible and that hinders my write-flow.

Approaching notepad is like talking to your best friend; no intimidation, no feeling of responsibility.
Word is like talking, pardon, trying to talk to your boss.
Enough said.
