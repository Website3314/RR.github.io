Again and Again

Again and again,
You arrive

We fight, we struggle, try to take each other down
we fail
we are on other levels
we're different

we are not opposites
you are not the fire to my water
nor am i the yin to your yang
We are not on the same level
simply unalike

like birds to bath tubs
like mice to scissors
uncomparable, not opposites
just different

the fight continues
I think this would defeat you
but it doesnt
our vast differences don't help
each assuming the other to be another
each failing to vanquish
and to be vanquished

we cannot come to a compromise
the fight goes on
we get tired
we shake hands and call it a day

the moon smiles at us
the sun reminds us what is to be done
predictable unpredicability
the fight begins
again and again

We cant take each other down
we dont know each other
but I am accustomed to your presence
as you are to mine

in the end one has to fall
the victor has to rise
we cannot defeat each other
we dont know each other

for if we knew
then one would surely emerge the victor
but we dont
we dont want to

as we try to conquer each other
again and again
we fail to realise
that first we must conquer ourselves

our pride, our ego
our fear of the different

I suppose,
we arent so different after all
we are alike
Cruel irony
